Privacy Policy

At Online Library, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or use our website and services.

We collect the following types of information from users:

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To Provide and Improve Services: We use your information to offer a personalized experience, troubleshoot issues, and improve the quality of our services.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and provide customer support.
  • Communication: To send you updates, newsletters, and notifications about your account, services, or important changes to our platform.
  • Analytics: To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activity for improving our services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in running our website or services (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers). These parties are required to keep your data secure and confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., government or law enforcement agencies).
